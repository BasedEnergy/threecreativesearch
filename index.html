What We Do

Three Creative Search is an executive search firm focused on placing Marketing, Design and Digital professionals on a direct-hire and interim basis. The professionals we place range in level of experience from Staff to C-level. We service a variety of industries, regardless of size. This includes anything from a start-up to a large publicly traded company and advertising agencies.

We identify the strongest talent in the marketplace through networking, direct recruiting, and referrals. We take the time to fully understand the needs of our clients, as well as the goals of our candidates. Throughout the interview process, we guide both sides to make it as seamless of an experience as possible.

Confidentiality is very important, whether you are a candidate that is currently working or an employer looking to keep the search confidential until the position is filled. When working with Three Creative Search, you can be rest assured that all information shared will be kept strictly confidential.

Our Goal

We seek to build long term relationships with both our candidates and clients. It's not just about making a placement today, but about helping candidates make the right move that fits their long-term goal.

Outreach

Our executive recruiters have a solid foundation in Creative Recruiting and extensive professional experience with a focus on customer service. We will never push a candidate into taking a job nor push a client into making a hire. It is our job to give professional advice and guidance based on our knowledge.

Candidates

We specialize in placing experienced marketing, design and digital professionals in a variety of industries. Your search will always be kept strictly confidential, and you will always have control over where your resume is sent. Regardless of your reason for considering a change, we will listen to your needs and do our best to place you in a position that fits your goals.

Employers

As our client, you can feel confident that the candidates presented to you have been interviewed and possess the qualifications of your open position. It is our job to save you time in the screening process and present the best possible candidate available in the marketplace. If we don't present the best candidate, our services are free.

Jesse Triplett - Recruiter

Jesse grew up on the central coast of California, where he got started working for large staffing companies in Santa Barbara. He also worked in Portland as the sole recruiter for one of the most exciting independent ad agencies in the country. He most recently worked as an executive/direct hire recruiter for one best staffing agencies in creative recruiting. Over the last 16 years, he’s worked on C-level searches - successfully - for large companies, entry level designers for small ad agencies and everything in between. In the last year he’s closed positions all over the US, in all time zones. He chose to start Three Creative Search because he wanted to do something different and change the way candidates and clients look at recruiters. He’s not in this for the big money ( though money puts food on the table ) and that has allowed him to put the people before the paycheck.

Katie Brien - Operations

Katie grew up in San Diego and shortly after graduating high school moved to Los Angeles, where she got her start working in the entertainment industry, acting in independent films, working as an extra on blockbuster films and attending college. Over the last 20 years, she has done it all, or at least everything she ever put her mind to. After hanging up the mic and stepping down from the loneliest place on earth - the stage - she went back to school at Portland State as a Film major, marrying her experience as an actor, stand up comedian and stylist, eventually relocating to Atlanta, GA to work in the film industry on the Production side.
