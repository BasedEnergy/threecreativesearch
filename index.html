Let a perfect match be made for you

Hello, we're Three Creative Search, your new premium recruiting service. We know that finding work or the right employee isn't easy and getting the perfect one is even harder. So let us take care of that, we're really good at it, we promise!

Outreach

The professionals we place, range in levels of experience from Staff to C-level. We service a variety of industries, regardless of size. This includes anything from a start-up to a large publicly traded company and advertising agencies.

Quick & effective

Be prepared to fill your coming schedule with interviews from all the firms or employees that match you. Our partners or applications are anxious to find you! Just search with us and wait.

Stand out

We identify the strongest talent in the marketplace through networking, direct recruiting, and referrals. Throughout the interview process, we guide both sides to make it as seamless of an experience as possible.

Confidentiality

Whether you are a candidate that is currently working or an employer looking to keep the search confidential until the position is filled, rest assured that all information shared will be kept strictly confidential.

Meet the team

Our executive recruiters have a solid foundation in Creative Recruiting and extensive professional experience with a focus on customer service. We will never push a candidate into taking a job nor push a client into making a hire. It is our job to give professional advice and guidance based on our knowledge.

Jesse Triplett

Jesse grew up on the central coast of California, where he got started working for large staffing companies in Santa Barbara. He also worked in Portland as the sole recruiter for one of the most exciting independent ad agencies in the country. He most recently worked as an executive/direct hire recruiter for one best staffing agencies in creative recruiting. Over the last 16 years, he’s worked on C-level searches - successfully - for large companies, entry level designers for small ad agencies and everything in between. In the last year he’s closed positions all over the US, in all time zones. He chose to start Three Creative Search because he wanted to do something different and change the way candidates and clients look at recruiters. He’s not in this for the big money ( though money puts food on the table ) and that has allowed him to put the people before the paycheck.
